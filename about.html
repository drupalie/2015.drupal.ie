About

The Drupal Ireland Association are pleased to invite everyone to Drupal Open Days 2015. Meet and learn from the best Drupal minds in Ireland bringing together developers, themers, end users and those interested in learning more about the platform. DrupalCamp Dublin takes place on the 22nd and 23rd of May at the Guinness Enterprise Centre, right next to the Guinness Storehouse.
