Drupal as a publishing platform for podcasts

Friday 22nd, 11:30
Presenter: 
conor
Session Level: 
Intermediate
Session Theme: 
Drupal Development

Drupal is a great content management framework for building an online media publishing platform. And usually it just requires another contributed module to add extra functionality. 

Very successful shows like Serial Podcast from the makers of This American Life, chose to build their website with Drupal 7. But there isn’t one tried and tested turn-key module for publishing podcasts, that then work well out of the box, with podcast aggregators like Apple iTunes.

This presentation will look at one essential module that can be the keystone for building and configuring a podcasting platform.

Some alternative platforms will be compared and one hybrid combination will be shared.

This presentation is aimed at publishers. No prior knowledge of Drupal is required.
